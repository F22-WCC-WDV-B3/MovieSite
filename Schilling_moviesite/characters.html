Guy - Played By: Ryan Reynolds

The Main Character of Free Guy. An AI who gained sentience due to the code developed by Keys and Millie. Blue Shirt Guy was given his name after gaining internet fame due to his interactions in Free City, with his original NPC name simply being "Guy"

Buddy - Played By: Lil Rel Howery

Blue Shirt Guy's best friend. Buddy is a police officer who spends most of his time with Blue Shirt Guy in the bank where they work. Buddy was present when Blue Shirt Guy realized his sentience. Buddy acts as a comedic relief character throughout most of the movie, but also a supportive friend for Blue Shirt Guy.

Walter "Keys" McKey - Played By: Joe Keery

One of the developers of the code that gave Blue Shirt Guy sentience. After a failed lawsuit against Antwan, Keys decided to work for him. He works as one of the main developers of Free City during the movie.

Millie/Molotov Girl - Played By: Jodie Comer

The second developer of the code that gave Blue Shirt Guy sentience. After a failed lawsuit against Antwan, Millie went into hiding to continue searching for evidence against him. She plays Free City under the username MolitovGirl, searching the game for the evidence she needs.

Antwan - Played By: Taika Waititi

The founder and CEO of Soonami Studios, developers of Free City. After stealing the code developed by Millie and Keys, Antwan uses it in Free City. Antwan is obsessed with profit and will do anything if it means making more money.
