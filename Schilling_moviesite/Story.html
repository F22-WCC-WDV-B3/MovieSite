The story of Free Guy follows the main character, Blue Shirt Guy, who is a sentient AI inside of the game Free City. His newfound sentience causes problems for the players of the game as he begins to interfere with gameplay.

This causes the developers to take notice and try to shut Guy down. Meanwhile, Guy meets a user by the name of MolotovGirl who teaches him how to play. Guy becomes a celebrity quickly with his passive and non-violent approach to progressing, which causes many problems for the developers.

Walter "Keys" McKey, one of the developers of Free City, meets up with Millie; the player behind MolotovGirl. He tells Millie that their sentient AI code worked and is the reason Guy exists.

Keys works with Millie to expose Antwan's thievery which turns into the climax of the movie.
